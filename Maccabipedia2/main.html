Maccabi Tel Aviv official fan page

Maccabi Tel Aviv is an Israeli sports association, belonging to the Maccabi Center and located in the city of Tel Aviv. The association, which was established in 1906 under the name - The "Association of the Jewish Gymnasts - the first to mark Jaffa" is considered the leading association in Israel, and its divisions have won many local championship titles and cups, and even won European championships and other international competitions. The association's athletes have an Olympic medal and notable international achievements in the various branches, including participation in the Olympic Games and the Maccabiah Games. In the early 1960s, the association was registered in the Golden Book of the National Fund for Israel.
